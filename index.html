About Us

We have a different mindset and approach, solving complex client problems while helping you shape your better future.
Oxelan Consulting PLC is a leading consultancy firm offering comprehensive services to national and international clients, including NGOs, governmental organizations, and private enterprises. Established in 2021, aiming to drive positive change through research, advisory, and training in organizational management, business development, investment, social enterprise support, and sustainable development. With a team of seasoned professionals in various fields, we provide tailored solutions to empower clients, guiding them toward sustainable success and impactful outcomes.

Comprehensive Research

At OXELAN we pride ourselves on our expertise in executing comprehensive surveys, studies, and monitoring and evaluation (M&E) frameworks. Our dedicated research team is committed to delivering high-quality, tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each project or organization.

Strategic Consulting

At OXELAN, we specialize in providing strategic consulting services that empower our clients to maximize the accuracy and reliability of their information systems. Our comprehensive approach encompasses qualitative and quantitative research, tailored monitoring and evaluation (M&E) systems, and extensive capacity building and logistical support.

Training and Mentorship

At OXELAN, we offer comprehensive training and mentorship services aimed at empowering both individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge necessary for success. Our diverse range of programs caters to various needs, encompassing everything from soft skills enhancement to specialized industry training and capacity development.

Business Development Services

Our business development services are meticulously crafted to catalyze fostering growth and facilitating expansion within your organization. We understand the importance of not only sustaining but also propelling your business forward in today's competitive landscape.
